Our Story

Rooted in Hanoi,
perfected in Orange County.

Phở Quang Trung began with one belief — that the honest, herb-bright cooking of Northern Vietnam belongs on every table. For more than three decades our head chef and owner has carried the recipes of a Hanoi home kitchen to the heart of Little Saigon.
