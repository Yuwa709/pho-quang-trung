Our Story

Rooted in Hanoi,
perfected in Orange County.

For more than three decades, one family has carried the herb-bright cooking of Northern Vietnam to the heart of Little Saigon — one bowl of phở at a time.

35+ Years on Bolsa Avenue

Phở Quang Trung began with one belief — that the honest, soulful cooking of Northern Vietnam belongs on every table. Our head chef and owner grew up in a Hanoi home kitchen, where broth simmered for half a day and nothing left the stove until it tasted like home.

When the family settled in Westminster, they brought those recipes with them — unchanged, uncompromised. More than thirty-five years later, we’re still on Bolsa Avenue, still making our American Wagyu phở broth from scratch every morning, still grilling pork over charcoal the way it’s done in the North.

We’re more than a place to eat. We’re a family — and when you sit down with us, so are you. Whether it’s a quiet solo bowl on a rainy afternoon or a long table set for a bus tour, every dish leaves our kitchen the way it would leave ours at home.
